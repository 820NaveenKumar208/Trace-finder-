

ProblemStatement:
High Financial Loss Due to Return Fraud
Online retailers face significant losses from fake damage claims, reused images, and fraudulent return requests.
Difficulty in Detecting Fraud at Scale
Manual verification is slow, inefficient, and not feasible for platforms handling millions of orders daily.
Balancing Fraud Detection with Customer Experience
Strict verification can frustrate genuine customers, while lenient policies increase fraud risk.

he cost of blocking a genuine customer is often greater than the cost of a single fraudulent return.


Proposed Solution
Develop an AI-powered real-time fraud detection system for return claims
Analyze uploaded product images using similarity detection (CLIP model)
Validate image metadata and detect inconsistencies
Evaluate user behavior patterns (return frequency, history)
Generate a Fraud Risk Score (0–100%)
Provide decision outputs: Auto Approve / Monitor / Manual Review
🚀 How Our Solution Is Better
⚡ Real-time detection — instant fraud evaluation without delays
🧠 AI-driven accuracy — detects reused or manipulated images effectively
😊 Frictionless experience — no need for video verification or extra steps
📊 Explainable decisions — shows reasons behind fraud score
🔧 Scalable design — can integrate into large e-commerce platforms
💥 Impact Statement
“Our solution reduces fraudulent return losses, minimizes manual verification effort, and preserves customer trust by enabling real-time, intelligent decision-making.”
